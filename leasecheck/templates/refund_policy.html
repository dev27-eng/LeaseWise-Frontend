Refund Policy

Our Refund Policy

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind our service with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your lease analysis, you can request a full refund within 30 days of your purchase.

Eligibility for Refund

  • Request must be made within 30 days of purchase
  • Service has not been fully utilized (lease analysis not completed)
  • Valid reason for dissatisfaction provided

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@coloradoleasecheck.com with your:

  • Order number
  • Reason for refund request
  • Date of purchase

Processing Time

Refund requests are typically processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Exceptions

Refunds may not be available if:

  • The 30-day period has expired
  • The lease analysis service has been fully utilized
  • The request violates our Terms of Service
